🚫 Cards to Avoid

A heuristic, not a guarantee. Route 7 flags cards showing the classic "looks premium, ages poorly" pattern: very high secret/hyper/gold rarity and a lowest listing sitting well under market (a sign supply outweighs demand). These are the cards seasoned collectors are wary of paying up for. Always cross-check with a market source like PokéBeard before deciding.
